## Budget Allocation Recommendations

### Google Ads Budget Distribution
- 60% High-Intent Search (sell apartment fast, cash for apartment)
- 30% Problem-Aware Search (foreclosure, inherited, tenants)
- 10% Competitor/Brand Terms

### Facebook/Instagram Budget Distribution
- 40% Cold Audience (targeting by demographics/interests)
- 30% Warm Audience (engaged with content, video views)
- 30% Hot Audience (website visitors, quiz abandoners)

## Tracking & Optimization

### Conversion Tracking Setup

**Google Ads Conversions:**
1. Quiz Started
2. Quiz Completed (Lead)
3. Phone Call
4. Deal Closed

**Facebook Pixel Events:**
1. PageView
2. ViewContent (landing page)
3. InitiateCheckout (quiz started)
4. Lead (quiz completed)
5. Purchase (deal closed)

### Key Metrics to Track

**Top of Funnel:**
- Impressions
- Click-through rate (CTR)
- Cost per click (CPC)
- Landing page visitors

**Mid Funnel:**
- Quiz start rate
- Quiz completion rate
- Cost per lead (CPL)
- Lead quality score

**Bottom Funnel:**
- Contact rate
- Offer acceptance rate
- Closing rate
- Cost per acquisition (CPA)
- Return on ad spend (ROAS)

### Optimization Checklist

**Week 1:**
- [ ] Monitor daily spend
- [ ] Check conversion tracking
- [ ] Review search terms (Google)
- [ ] Adjust bids for top performers

**Week 2:**
- [ ] Pause underperforming ads
- [ ] Test new ad copy variations
- [ ] Expand successful keywords
- [ ] Adjust audience targeting

**Week 3:**
- [ ] Analyze lead quality
- [ ] Optimize for conversion value
- [ ] Test new ad creatives
- [ ] Expand budget on winners

**Week 4:**
- [ ] Full performance review
- [ ] Calculate true ROAS
- [ ] Plan next month's strategy
- [ ] Scale successful campaigns
